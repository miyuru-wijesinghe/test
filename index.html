At VSM (NI), we have 20 years of experience in the industry & work hard to provide you with a first class service at all times. We are a rally car specialist, so if you're a motorsports enthusiast, give us a call today. We have access to manufacturer service schedules & technical data, so this allows us to carry out the same level of service but at a much lower price compared to a main dealer. Car Diagnostics
in Eglinton

When there is a problem with your vehicle, its on-board computer will store trouble codes & a warning light will appear on the dashboard display. We can then use our diagnostic equipment to scan the ECU for trouble codes. Every fault has its own unique code, sometimes there are multiple codes stored & these have to be eliminated in order for us to find the true cause of the problem. This will then enable us to find the information required to fix the problem.
